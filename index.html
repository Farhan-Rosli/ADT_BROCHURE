User Based Insurance (UBI)

User-Based Insurance (UBI) leverages advanced behavioral analytics, GPS technology, and CAN bus data to monitor driving habits in real time. By analyzing key vehicle parameters such as speed, braking, acceleration, and fuel efficiency, we provide a comprehensive assessment of driving behavior.
